Digital Temperature sensor SIGFOX IP68

The SIGFOX IP68 digital thermometer is designed for temperature measurement in the food industry. The temperature sensor is located in the food hose, so it is possible to measure the temperatures in an environment where the emphasis is on cleanliness.


    Technical parameters:
  • Temperature measurement in the range -55 to +155 ° C
  • Measurement resolution 0.1 ° C
  • Measuring accuracy +/- 0.5 ° C
  • Battery power
  • Very low power consumption in sleep mode (only 4uA
  • Long battery life (about 5 years)
  • Class A sensor
  • Protection IP 68
  • Remotely adjustable measurement period
  • Support abp / otaa
