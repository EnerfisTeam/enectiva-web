Cost-saving projects

By remotely collecting data in real-time, Enectiva automatically assesses economic parameters of cost-saving projects. Instead of dealing with tedious administration of energy-related data, you simply receive reports, as well as alerts, from Enectiva in form of text messages or e-mails.
