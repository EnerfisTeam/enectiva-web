Hardware

Enectiva is a system which is open to all manufacturers of M2M and Smart-metering hardware who meet our criteria in terms of utility value and reliability. Below, you can find hardware which has been tested by Enectiva and which is used by Enectiva users.

Are you an M2M hardware manufacturer? Work with us

2N SmartCom ft. Enectiva

A simpler version of a dependable, widely used concentrator SmartCom PRO manufactured by the 2N Telekomunikace company. The firmware has been adjusted for the purpose of the Enectiva ecosystem and it enables you to connect the concentrator to the Enectiva server in a plug-and-play fashion. All you need to do is to connect meters. The communication with the server can be realized through SIM cards or through Ethernet. Supported interfaces: RS232,M-bus,RS485,S0.

2N SmartCom PRO ft. Enectiva

The most widely used data concentrator in the Enectiva system. The firmware of this version is adjusted so that the communication between meters and the Enectiva server is smooth and does not require setting up of the individual interfaces. If you order the concentrator from our company, you just connect it to the power grid and it starts to communicate with the Enectiva server immediately. After connecting the meters, you are ready to use all the services of the Enectiva energy management. All types of meters using various interfaces may be connected to the SmartCom PRO concentrator. Supported interfaces: Wireless M-bus, ZigBee, RS485, RS232, M-bus a S0.

Amber wireless

Amber wireless is a very simple yet dependable transmitter operating in the Mbus Wireless standard, which can be connected to any meter with a pulse output (S0 output). This is typically an electric, gas or water meter. The transmitter converts the pulse output to a wireless radio transmission, frequency 868MHz, which is then further processed in the concentrator.
