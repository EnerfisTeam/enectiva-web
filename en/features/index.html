Detailed Consumption and Costs Statistics

With Enectiva, all your data concerning consumption will be neatly arranged and organized in one place. In this way, you can monitor the overall energy consumption in the building (gas, cold water, hot water, heat, cold, electric power – separate for the ventilation system, lighting, appliances, or cold generation operation).

You can display for example:

  • Proportional Graphs – they show the influence of energy on the final costs, the energy usage of various parts of the building compared to the overall consumption of the building, or the influence of the occupants, facilities and unmeasured consumption.
  • Comparative Graphs – they serve to compare the usage of two or more parts on the same level (buildings, parts of buildings, occupants, facilities,…). The benchmarking of the comparable parts of the portfolio enables you to discover buildings or occupants that differ conspicuously from the others and thus detect needless waste of energy.
    {{ "detailed statistics 2" | gallery_link: "podrobne statistiky_2", 1 }}
  • Monthly Consumption – you can see the development of the usage of individual energy resources during the year. Moreover, you can combine the graph with the development of temperature and thus control if the development of consumption corresponds to the climatic conditions in a given location. Thanks to the integrated price lists of all utilities, you can switch these graphs also to a final costs diagram.
    {{ "detailed statistics 3" | gallery_link: "podrobne statistiky_3", 1 }}
  • Development Graphs – they show the cumulative sum of the consumption or costs. In this way, you can very easily display the growth rate of your consumption during the monitored period.

Automatic Reports

You can easily set an unlimited number of automatic reports in Enectiva. Two basic types of reports are available:

  1. Regular Time Report
    After setting the time interval, this type of report will automatically send the data chosen by you directly to your e-mail. The extent and make-up of the data in the report depends only on you. You can add consumption and costs data to the report in the form of both charts and graphs. Each user can customize his report make-up and receive only data that are of interest or importance to him. Thus, you do not have to go through large amount of data and look for those relevant to you.

  2. Emergency Notice
    The job of an emergency notice is to inform you that a certain critical point has been reached. As with the regular reports, you decide what Enectiva should monitor. It can control, for example, the exceeding of a certain consumption or costs figure, the necessity to inspect some part of the equipment or a measuring device, the change of outside temperature, or (thanks to Enectiva’s connection to energy exchanges) even the development of energy prices on the PEX and EEX exchanges.

Saving Analysis

If you realize any energy project on your building or if you introduce cost-saving measures, the second and no less important phase of such a project follows – the monitoring of appreciation, and optimization. Not even the best design of a project or measure can cover all factors influencing the development of the project’s appreciation. Thus, the monitoring and assessment of the progress, and the following optimization are indispensable for all such projects.

Enectiva helps you with this task. It makes all calculations for you and, on one single page, you can easily see the consumption development and its comparison with the average consumption during the last three years. You can further see the monthly savings made by the measure and the development of and prediction for the financial side of the project, using the cash flow method based on economic parameters. As a result, you can easily find out whether the project is still in the stage of repaying the investment or of profitability, and also the current estimated NPV value at the end of the project’s lifespan.

Meteodata

Enectiva is connected to the network of weather stations all around the Czech Republic. When entering the input data of a building, you chose the weather station nearest to it. Enectiva will then receive its climatic conditions information, which you can display in a single graph with the consumption of individual energy resources. Thus, you can easily control whether the energy used on heating and cooling corresponds to the outside climatic conditions. Such comparison easily detects mistakes in the regulation setting of the mentioned systems.

Google Maps Support

Do you need to quickly look up information on several different buildings in your portfolio? Forget the annoying searching through many archived files. In Enectiva, you can switch from one building to another with only three clicks, thanks to its connection to Google Maps.

Unlimited Number of Users under a Licence

Enectiva’s licence system is unique on the Czech market mainly due to its applicability to a number of buildings and meters that are monitored. The number of users or access PCs is not important. You determine the number of user accounts yourself directly in the Enectiva interface according to your immediate needs, without any of the changes influencing your licence invoice.

Another advantage of this kind of licence is that there is no need to contact the sales team in order to increase or decrease the number of user accounts, and to deal with the related paperwork. The result is a faster administration of user accounts that responds immediately to your current needs and the company’s structure.

Easy Input of Meter Reading Data

Meter reading data can be put into Enectiva either manually or they can be sent in via a remote device. For manual input you can use a simple form right within Enectiva or (for example to input older meter reading data) a prepared form in the .xls format (MS Excel). In this way, for example, you can easily copy data from an existing chart to the model chart we prepared and, after you load the file, Enectiva will process the data.

Automatic Remote Meter Reading Support

Data can be put into Enectiva also by means of remote meter reading. This allows you to save the time spent by your technician every month reading the meters manually.

Enectiva is prepared for a wide range of hardware products that enable remote meter reading. Whether the data are collected on a wire or wireless basis is not important. Similarly, the producer and the type of the remote meter, or the data bus on the basis of which it works, do not matter. The basis for the correct functioning of Enectiva are the right data at the right time. Therefore, the system itself can take the data from your internal data storage or they can be sent through the hardware of your choice directly to your server, where they will be recorded and processed.

User Rights Hierarchy

Every company has a hierarchy of workers according to their authorities. Enectiva reflects this fact by the possibility of assigning each user authorities within the system. Thus, by setting the type of account, you can easily determine for each user what he can view and edit in the system.

At present, we respect the following types of users:
Administrator, Technician, Occupant, Facility manager, Property manager, Asset manager, Portfolio manager, Owner. All these users are distinguished by different rights and options within the system.

Integrated Utilities Price Lists

Enectiva contains the price lists of all utility distributors active in the Czech Republic. You choose your distributor in the system, put in the unit price for the utility arranged with the supplier, and Enectiva will automatically count the overall costs according to the formulas given by ERO (Energy Regulatory Office).

Of course, an update is made by our development team every time a new price decision is issued by ERO.

Easy Control

During the development of the user interface, our team focused on maximum ease and intuitiveness of Enectiva. All functions and controls are designed in such a way that, after a short initial training, each user gets quickly familiar with Enectiva. An inspiration during the development of the user interface and its functions were, for example, products of the Google corporation.

Easy Management of Meters

In Enectiva, meters are included in the floor plans of a building. Therefore, you can easily display the individual floors of a building and edit the meters directly from the plans. This saves you the toilsome searching through documents and charts in order for you or your technician to locate the various meters.

{{ 'Drag&Drop function' | gallery_link: 'drag_drop_1', 10, 'feature__image' }}

Drag&Drop Function

Enectiva tries to solve any task in the most simple and elegant way possible. The Drag&Drop function enables you to move all data to a newly selected higher category by simply grabbing and dragging an occupant, a building, or a complex.

For example, if you manage a portfolio of more investors and one buys a real estate from the other, you transfer all data about this real estate into the new owner’s portfolio with a single move.

{{ Facility management 1 | gallery_link: 'facility management_1', 11 }}

Facility Management

Enectiva contains the basic features of facility management such as the following parameters: “total floor area”, “gross floor area”, or “gross leasable area”. These areas can be distinguished in Enectiva according to their use (for example as offices, storerooms, facilities, retail, etc.)

Similarly, you can input the number of occupants of the object, parking spots, kitchenettes, WCs, urinals and the like. All these parameters are divided into the overall number and the currently occupied or used ones.

As a result, Enectiva can recalculate the consumption and costs to the specific consumption or specific costs according to the selected criterion. The data are archived and you can view the time development of these parameters at any time in the future.

Events

You can enter any change that occurs in your building or portfolio into Enectiva (for example, the heat/cold source replacement, a change of the energy supplier, a change of the energy price, windows replacement, thermal insulation of the object, etc.)

You can then display these changes in the monthly consumption graphs. In a simple and fast way, you can see the change in the consumption and costs development due to these events.

Connection to the PEX and EEX Exchanges

Thanks to its connection to the PEX and EEX energy exchanges, you can follow the development of electric power and gas prices directly from the Enectiva interface. In this way, you can easily watch the prices development trend, and invite tenders for an energy supplier at a convenient moment. Using the automatic reports, you can also set Enectiva to send you a notice as soon as the energy price reaches the desired value.
